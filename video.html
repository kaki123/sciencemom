Free Play

For this part of the study, we are hoping to explore the different ways that mothers and their children have fun together! We are interested in looking at how you and your child interact with one another during a brief period of free play.

Free Play Instructions

Without using any toys or props, please spend 3 to 5 minutes playing one-on-one with your child, and we would like you to record this time you spend together.

Try to find a space with no distractions and simply use your mobile device to record! Please refer to the steps below on how to share your video with our team.
