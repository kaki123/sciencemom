We are researchers at the Claremont Colleges and Harvard Medical School, who are interested in understanding children’s development. We are going to the source, YOU. Help us understand how personality, biology, and parenting shape children’s psychological and physical well-being.


Eligibility

To be a scientist mommy, you need to:

Have a child between the ages of 3 and 5 years old and have internet access.

We will ask you to help us collect data by participating in four activities:

  • Activity 1: Surveys - We will ask about basic demographics, your parenting style and your child's personality. The surveys take about 30 minutes.

  • Activity 2: Cognitive games - We will use computerized games to test your memory and planning.

  • Activity 3: Video clip - We want to "virtually" meet you and your child! We will ask you to record a short video clip of you and your child playing together so that we can capture the way you interact with one another.

  • Activity 4: Hair sample - We will ask you to send us a small sample of you and your child's hair to measure stress levels.


Compensation (Reward)

Not only will you help us understand how personality, biology, and parenting shape children’s psychological and physical well-being, but you will also receive a $50 Amazon gift card for participating in this study. You will also have to option of donating this giftcard to your child's preschool.
