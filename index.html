Scientist Mommy

A study to help us understand how personality, biology, and parenting shape children’s psychological well-being, and physical health.

We are researchers at the Claremont Colleges and Harvard Medical School, who are interested in understanding children’s development. And we are going to the source, YOU.

To be a scientist mom, you need to:

  • Have a child between the ages of 3-5 years old

    • We will ask you to help us collect data using:

    1. Surveys

    2. Computerized games (to test memory and planning)

    3. Biological samples (small samples of hair and nail clippings)

    4. Video clips of you interacting and playing with your child.


Conpensation (Reward)

Not only will you help us understand how personality, biology, and parenting shape children’s psychological well-being, and physical health, but you will receive a $100 Amazon or Target gift card for participating in this study.

All data will be secured, and kept confidential. Your name and your child’s name will never be used, it will never be linked to the data in any public manner. Only certified researchers, will have access to anonymized data.
