Why Do We Need Surveys?

The first activity of Scientst Mommy is simply for you to fill out some questionnaires. When you click on ENROLL, you will be ask to provide us with your contact information, consent to the study, and fill out a couple of short questionnaires about yourself.

Questionnaires

After the enrolling in the study, we will send you a personalized link to a series of questionnaires.

  • The questionnaires ask about: your emotional experience and your parenting style.
  • They take about 30 minutes to complete.
  • You are welcome to skip any questions.

Remember all of the data is kept completely confidential. Your responses will NOT be linked to your name or email address. We will NOT share any of your information.

Approximately one week after you complete this set of surveys, we will follow up with you through an email that will outline Activity 3 and Activity 4!
