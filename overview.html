    1. Surveys

    2. Computerized games (to test memory and planning)

Collecting Hair Samples

A primary goal of this study is to compare levels of stress between you and your child by examining the cortisol that can be measured in hair. By looking at this stress hormone among dyads, we hope to learn more about the two of you on a biological level! Please follow the steps described below for both you and your child and send your samples to the following address: Stacey Doan Claremont McKenna College 850 Columbia Ave.Claremont, CA 91711

Uploading a Video Onto Youtube

For this part of the study, we are hoping to explore the different ways that mothers and their children have fun together! We are interested in looking at how you and your child interact with one another during a brief period of free play. Without using any toys or props, please spend 3 to 5 minutes playing one-on-one with your child, and we would like you to record this time you spend together. Try to find a space with no distractions and simply use your mobile device to record! Please refer to the steps below on how to share your video with our team.
