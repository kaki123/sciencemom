Why Do We Need Surveys?

These surveys are for us to know as much about you while being as comfortable as possible.

Questionnaires

There are two rounds of surveys that you will complete:

  • You will provide consent and describe basic demographic & health information
  • Then, we will email you a personalized link to a series of questionnaires asking about your emotional experiences, as well as aspects of your relationship with your child
  • By starting this survey, you agree that you have a child between the ages of 3 and 5 years old.

Start Here!

Approximately one week after you complete this set of surveys, we will follow up with you through an email that will outline the steps you will be taking for the next part of the study!
