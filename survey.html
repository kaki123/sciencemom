Why Do We Need Surveys?

The first activity of Scientist Mommy is simply for you to fill out some questionnaires. When you click on ENROLL, you will be asked to provide us with your contact information, consent to the study, and answer a couple of questions about yourself.

Questionnaires

After we confirm that you qualify for this study, we will send you a personalized link to a series of questionnaires.

  • You will be asked about your experience during the coronavirus/COVID-19 pandemic and your parenting style.
  • The surveys should take approximately 45-60 minutes in total to complete.
  • You are welcome to skip any question you are not comfortable answering.

Remember all of the data is kept completely confidential. Your responses will NOT be linked to your name or email address. We will NOT share any of your information.
