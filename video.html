How to Upload a Video Onto Youtube

Uploading a Video Onto Youtube

For this part of the study, we are hoping to explore the different ways that mothers and their children have fun together! We are interested in looking at how you and your child interact with one another during a brief period of free play. Without using any toys or props, please spend 3 to 5 minutes playing one-on-one with your child, and we would like you to record this time you spend together. Try to find a space with no distractions and simply use your mobile device to record! Please refer to the steps below on how to share your video with our team.

HOW TO UPLOAD VIDEOS TO YOUTUBE WITH AN ANDROID PHONE

As a Google account holder, you also have a YouTube account. You can use the YouTube app on your Android phone to upload videos to the Internet. Here’s how:

  1. Activate the phone’s Wi-Fi.

  2. From the Apps Menu screen, choose the Gallery app.

  3. View the video you want to upload. Or simply have the video displayed on the screen.

  4. Touch the Share button, and choose YouTube from the menu.

  5. Touch the More Details button and type a description, specify to make the video private.

  6. Touch the Upload button.

You can return to the Gallery as the video is being uploaded. It continues to upload, even if the phone gets bored and falls asleep.

To view your video, open the YouTube app on the App menu, press the Menu soft button, and choose the My Channel command. If necessary, choose your Google account from the pop-up list. Your video should appear in the Uploads list.

HOW TO UPLOAD IPHONE VIDEOS TO YOUTUBE VIA CAMERA ROLL

As a Google account holder, you also have a YouTube account.

  1. Go to Camera Roll and select the video you want to upload.

  2. FTap on the share icon from the bottom left corner.

  3. View the video you want to upload. Or simply have the video displayed on the screen.

  4. Touch the Share button, and choose YouTube from the menu.

  5. Touch the More Details button and type a description, specify to make the video private.

  6. Touch the Upload button.

You can return to the Gallery as the video is being uploaded. It continues to upload, even if the phone gets bored and falls asleep.

To view your video, open the YouTube app on the App menu, press the Menu soft button, and choose the My Channel command. If necessary, choose your Google account from the pop-up list. Your video should appear in the Uploads list.
